Filter / Head Start:

Experimental filter / highlighter. Select one or more checkboxes below to show only the categories selected. And, some higher priority enchantments (based on community feedback) will highlight in different shades depending on how important they are in the categories selected.

×

Help!

Labels including augment colors, can be clicked to toggle collapsing them. For example if you are not interested in orbs or yellow augment slots, click to hide them.

Some enchantments, like 'Potency' or 'Spell Lore (Universal)' only fit in one or two spots. So always select and place your most important enchantments first.

Use the filter (under the gear icon) to highlight difficult to place and/or important enchantments for your class(es). Use the save / open buttons at the bottom to preserve your work.
